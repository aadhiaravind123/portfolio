AppInventor-Paper Toss

September 2017-October 2017

The objective of the AppInventor project was to create a game using inspiration from the tutorials given. My partner,Jason Tran, and I chose Mini-Golf as our tutorial. We decided to create a paper toss game as it uses the same flinging mechanic as the Mini-golf. We also decided to make two levels each that gets progressively harder than the previous one. I learned how to use the appinventor servers and how you can work with team members well. Some of the challenges that we had was that our game usually the levels sometimes broke and it took a long time for our score and attempts to work. We worked well together as we incorporated both of our ideas into the game. Also, we split the work equally like how we each did some work and then compared what we did and added what was good.
